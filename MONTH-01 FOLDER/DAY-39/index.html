Dear Dad,

You have been the quiet strength behind so many ordinary days that turned into beautiful memories because you were there. You may not always ask for attention, but your care is felt in every sacrifice, every protective silence, and every moment you stood like a wall for your family.

Sunny Chawla, your story began on 12 November 1984, but your real legacy lives in the comfort you create around you. A father's love is often not loud; it shows up in long workdays, worried eyes, practical advice, and the way home feels calmer when you are around.
